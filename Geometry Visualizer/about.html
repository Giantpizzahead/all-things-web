Geometry Is Important.

Geometry is apparant no matter where we look; the shape of the chair you're sitting in, along with almost every other piece of furniture, was carefully designed with the help of geometric tools. Even the walls, floor, and ceiling are all geometric (they're rectangular prisms!). So, being able to visualize geometry is an extremely important and useful tool.
